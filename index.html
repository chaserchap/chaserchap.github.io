Ever since I was little I've tried to read patterns into everyday life, it wasn't until I was older that I realized I could do this for a living.

In third grade I was banished to the hall with another student and a fourth grade math book, and ever since math came easy to me. From earning a Higher Level Certificate in Mathematics from the International Baccalaureate to completing a Master's in Mathematics, with a focus on Applied Abstract Algebra and courses in Statistical Theory, ANOVA and Design of Experiments and Applied Mathematics. Since July 2014 I have honed my skills in data science, working around deployment schedules to gain a Specialization in Data Science from Johns Hopkin's through Coursera. I have earned certificates in Statistical Inference, Regression Models, Developing Data Products and more completing each with distinction. I expect to complete this specialization January 2016.

Since working on my Master's I have been a United States Navy Submarine Officer. This unique opportunity has taught me many important lessons, the most important of which being leadership. The ability to focus a watch team and direct them efficiently and effectively is not an ability that comes with rank or title, but must be earned. I was not evaluated as the number one Junior Officer onboard the best vessel in the Atlantic Fleet for demanding respect, but for earning it. This is further exemplified in the two Navy and Marine Corps Achievement Medals I received for my work as a watchstander and in coordinating a massive Material and Preservation Inspection. Leading a team of unique individuals with highly varied skills is not only a skill I have cultivated, but an endeavour for which I have a great passion.

I can bring many unique and varied skills to your company. I'm a quick learner with an aptitude for coding languages, currently utilizing R, Python, MySQL, HTML and CSS for various projects. As a highly analytical thinker I can easily parse relevant information from a dataset, and have the coding and data wrangling skills to do so efficiently. I love a challenge and the opportunity to explore and expand my knowledge. Whether as part of a team or leading one, I am diligent in creating a work environment where myself and others can thrive. I look forward to the opportunity to show your company what I can accomplish for you.
